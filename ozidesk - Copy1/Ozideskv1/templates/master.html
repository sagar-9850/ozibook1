Frequently Asked Questions

How is the work culture at Ozibook?

Ozibook promotes healthy team work wherein views of every intern are valued and taken into consideration. Fruitful team discussions in regular meetings, flexible timings, easy to approach team leads and management, versatility in tasks assigned, appreciation for initiatives and innovative approach to a problem will make you more committed to your responsibilities at Ozibook

What is the duration of internship?

Duration of internship is 2 months. Internship may be extended or converted into full time employment based on your performance.

How long can I extend my internship period?

Ozibook is always open for talented and dedicated minds. You can remain with our company for an extended period as you wish, provided that your dedication to the company remains steadfast and the quality of your work continues to meet the expected standards.

What are the working days and number of hours to work per day?

Ozibook gives flexibility of working at your own pace and time but expects you to be committed to tasks and their deadlines. As long as you complete & deliver your work before time, you’re good. If the task requires you to put in 4 hours a day, it’s up to you when and how to put them.

What are the expectations from interns?

You need to be proactive and should be proficient in the respective technical areas of your role. You are required to learn things from time to time when the task demands. There will be collaboration between different departments so you are expected to learn the basics of how every department functions.

Will I get a stipend and a letter of recommendation?

Yes. The perks include Certificate, Letter Of Recommendation (Based on performance), Stipend and flexible work hours.
